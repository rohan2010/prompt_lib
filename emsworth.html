Why EMSWORTH

Built on the
details.

Four principles. Every product, every collection, without exception.

🛡️

Anti-Microbial Finishing

Every EMSWORTH towel product undergoes our proprietary anti-microbial finishing process — inhibiting bacterial growth and keeping your textiles fresher for longer, wash after wash.

100%
⚖️

GSM 450 Standard

Our benchmark weight delivers the perfect balance of absorbency and quick-drying performance — substantial enough to feel luxurious, light enough to dry between uses.

450
GSM
🌿

Organic & Natural Fibres

From certified organic cotton in the Luxe collection to bamboo-cotton blends in Safari — every fibre is chosen for its relationship with skin and the environment.

🧶

Yarn-Dyed Craftsmanship

Yarn-dyed 2/20s Combed Ringspun cotton means colour is locked in at the fibre level — patterns stay vivid, wash-fast, and dimensionally stable across years of use.

💨

Zero Twist Pile Technology

The Feather Nest collection uses zero-twist cotton pile — untwisted yarns create an airier, more voluminous loop that traps warmth and moisture with extraordinary gentleness. Designed for newborns, perfected for everyone.
